LET ME INTRODUCE MYSELF

I’m Kenneth, a Mechanical Engineer with 5 years of hands-on experience in automotive design and technical support. My primary design tool is Catia, where I create accurate and manufacturable 3D models and 2D drawings for resin and metal components.

I also have experience in Beta Cae, working on geometry cleanup, midsurface extraction, meshing and quality checks to support basic CAE and simulation requirements. In addition, I use SolidWorks for simple 3D modeling and basic analysis to assist in design validation.

I focus on clean design data, manufacturability, and practical solutions that help projects move efficiently from concept to production. Let’s work together to turn your ideas into reality.
